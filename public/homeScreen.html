About Advanta Investment

Breaking Barriers to Premium Investments

For nearly a century, regulatory restrictions have limited individual investors’ access to private markets, granting billion-dollar institutions exclusive opportunities. This imbalance has left most investors confined to public markets, excluding them from high-growth private investment opportunities like real estate and venture capital. Today, technology is reshaping this outdated system. That’s where Avanta Wealth comes in. As America’s leading direct-to-consumer private markets manager, we’ve developed a cutting-edge technology platform to eliminate these barriers. Our software empowers us to operate at the scale of institutional investors while avoiding unnecessary bureaucracy. By combining advanced technology with deep investment expertise, we’re redefining access to private markets and building smarter, more diversified portfolios for our clients.
