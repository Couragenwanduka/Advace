Meet the Team

Benjamin Cooper

President & Founding Partner

Benjamin Cooper is the President and Founding Partner of Advanta Wealth Limited, bringing nearly three decades of experience in the financial services industry. Throughout his career, Benjamin has held prominent roles at leading UK-based financial institutions, including Barclays Wealth, Schroders, and Lloyds Private Banking.

Born and raised in the United Kingdom, Benjamin pursued his education at prestigious institutions such as the London School of Economics and the University of Oxford, specializing in business finance and mathematics. This strong academic background provided the foundation for his expertise in wealth management and financial strategy.

Benjamin has earned key professional certifications, including the FCA-regulated equivalents of the Series 7, 24, 63, and 65 Licenses, solidifying his role as a trusted advisor in the UK financial sector. As an FCA-Registered Principal and Branch Manager, he has led teams and developed bespoke financial strategies for high-net-worth individuals and businesses.

A lifelong learner, Benjamin dedicates significant time to continuing professional development, focusing on advanced wealth management techniques and complex case design.

Benjamin is passionate about helping investors achieve their financial goals, offering personalized solutions that ensure long-term security and success.
